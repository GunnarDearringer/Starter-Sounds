Beginner Guitar FAQs

  • Do I need an amp to start playing guitar?
    Not always! You can start unplugged or use headphone amps or practice apps.
  • What size guitar is best for beginners?
    Full-size for teens/adults, 3/4 size for younger players.
  • What type of guitar should I start with?
    Electric guitars are easier on the fingers, but acoustics are great for portability and simplicity.
